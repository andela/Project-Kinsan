CARDS FOR HUMANITY

A game for horrible people desperately trying to do good

HOW TO PLAY

Each player begins with, and will always have, 10 white answer cards.
A CZAR randomly picks a black card containing a question to which you submit an answer to.
The CZAR choose an answer submitted
The person whose answer get selected by the CZAR scores a point
You need five point to win the game.
